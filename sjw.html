Social Justice Warrior

Social Justice Warriors, commonly referred to as as SJWs, are people who defend other people in order to have some form of personal benefit. They generally defend those who are played as 'victims' in the case of social media. They defend them through the acts of using threats, DDoSing, and denying evidence in order to claim false as true.
