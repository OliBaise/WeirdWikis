Beyonce

"Beyonce Knowles" is a young American pop singer and songwriter in the group Destiny's Child.

In 2002, she won the title "Songwriter of the Year", from the ASCAP Pop Music Awards. She is the first African American woman to win the award and the second woman.

She appeared in a film in 2002 called "Austin Powers as Goldmember".
