Reggaeton

Reggaeton has been in Puerto Rico for over 10 years. Most of the singers are male, but the few girls are bad*ss. The style is a mix from Rap & Hip Hop with our native music "Bomba" and "Plena" wich gives the regular rap a latin touch and way more nice to dance... in a very sexy way. Think about 96. bps approx. The lyrics are about the reality on the streets, missunderstandings, unfair situations, and last but not least about love, cheating, and been really mad at someone because of something.
