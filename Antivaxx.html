AntiVaxx

Anti-vaccine activism is organized activity designed to increase vaccine hesitancy, often by disseminating assertions that have been identified as misinformation or disinformation.

Anti-vaccine activism has historically been apolitical, but in the 2010s and 2020s the movement in the United States has increasingly targeted conservatives.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, anti-vaccine activists in several made attempts to hinder people who wanted to receive the vaccines, including attempting to blockade vaccination sites and making false reservations for vaccination appointments.
