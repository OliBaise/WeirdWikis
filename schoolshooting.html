School Shooting

AThe term school massacre refers to an incident of mass murder occuring at a school, normally perpetrated by students, although also in some cases by outsiders. School massacres in recent history have included:
    the killings in Erfrut, Germany in 2002 the Columbine High School massacre in 1999 the Dunblane massacre in 1996
