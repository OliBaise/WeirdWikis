Adele

Adele is an English Soul/Jazz singer from South London. She is currently signed to indepentdent music label XL Recordings. At the moment, Adele play small gigs in venues all over Britain and has not yet released any singles or albums. She has become quite popular on Myspace where you can here her songs such as Daydreamer, Best For Last, Home Town Glory and Crazy For You. She has already performed Daydreamer on Jools Holland.
