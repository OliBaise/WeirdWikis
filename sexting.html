Sexting

"Sexting" is the act of performing sexual acts with another person while using text messages.

It is considered by many to be the "lowest" form of sexual relations, just a step under internet cyber sex. This is becuase it is performed with abbreviations such as "u", "y", "r", ect.

History

The term "Sexting" was origonally coined by Eric Ketteman, also known as "Skip" while driving in his car, refering to how lovers keep intimate over long distance.
