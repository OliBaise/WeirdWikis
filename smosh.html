Smosh

www.smosh.com is a fantastic website created by Anthony Padilla. It is a Flash Portal as well as a Forum. It has over 5000 members and is growing every day. Anthony and his friend Ian have created many funny music videos based on old cartoons and television shows, and the flash animation, Die Smoshing ep. 1. If you are a flash artist, you can submit your animations to the Flash Gateway and it will be rated by the smosh members.
