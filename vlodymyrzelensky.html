Vlodymyr Zelenskyy

Volodymyr Oleksandrovych Zelensky (Template:Lang-ua; born 25 January 1978) is a Ukrainian showman, screenwriter, actor, art-director of "Kvartal 95".

He studied Law at the Kyiv National Economic University. He was a member of the board and the general producer of the TV channel "Inter" from 2010 to 2012.
