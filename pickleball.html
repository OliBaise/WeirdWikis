Pickleball

Pickleball is a paddle sport created in Banbridge Island Washington. It is best described as a cross between Tennis and Ping Pong.

It is played on a court, about 2/3 the size of a Tennis court. A ball similar to a wiffle ball, only with more, smaller holes is used. Traditionally wooden paddles are used and often light weight fiber glass padles are used.
