Napoleon Dynamite

Napoleon Dynamite (2004) An indie film, Our hero Napoleon (Jon Heder) is a carrot-topped high schooler living in rural Idaho with a penchant for funk music and admiration for the ninja way of life. All he wants is a little respect from the ladies, but his offbeat circle of friends and family keep getting in the way. This loveable nerd often uses words like "GOSH!" and "T.O.ed". Also co-stars Efren Ramirez as the sterotypical hispanic sidekick, Pedro. Napoleon and Pedro win the day when Pedro is elected class president thanks in part to Dynamite's dance moves and Napoleon sucessfully gets rid of his medling Uncle Rico.
