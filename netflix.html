Netflix

Netflix is one of increasing online DVD rental services. It provides month flat-fee service where you can rent unlimited numbers of DVD discs with certain numbers of out. Unlike traditional DVD rental shops, there is no due dates, or late fees, no per-movie rental fees.

At present (2003), netflix offers three type of monthly programs
  • $39.95 Netflix Ultimate - up to 8 movies out at a time
  • $29.95 Netflix Plus - up to 5 movies out at a time
  • $19.95 Netflix Standard - up to 3 movies out at a time
  • $13.95 Netflix Economy - up to 4 rentals a month and up to 2 movies out at a time


Netflix is a example about the odd situation about copyright issues on the Internet.
