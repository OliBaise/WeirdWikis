Sandstorm

"Sandstorm" is a 12" and a CD single by the Finnish DJ Darude. The single was released by Neo in 1999. This anthemic production by Darude has become a major staple of electronic music. It is considered one of the most widely known songs of the genre. The high energy beat and melody contrasts well with the slow opening. This single is one of the all-time best selling electronica singles. The song was featured in the popular dance simulation game Dance Dance Revolution. The song was also featured in the hit Wesley Snipes action film Blade and comedy Fun with Dick and Jane.
