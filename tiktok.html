TikTok Short Video

Tik Tok Short Video (Chinese: 抖音短视频; Pinyin: Douyin) is officially launched in 2016 by Toutiao. It is a Mobile App that allow creators to quickly and easily make an unique short video, which is basically between 15-60 seconds, to share with friends and the world. Its special effects include shaking and jiving to hip-hop and electronic music, changing hair color, 3D stickers, and other props. In addition, creators can take their talent to the next level and tap into a massive music library.
