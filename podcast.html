Podcast

Podcasting is based on asynchronous bundles of passion, automatically delivered to your device of choice while you sleep or at any other time you desire. They must be a discrete and downloadable media file. They are published in an RSS 2.0 enclosure feed. They are then handled automatically on the receiver end, downloaded and moved to where it needs to be and put in the playlists for your playback device. It is meant to rhyme with broadcasting and is a derivative of the ipod platform. While not directly associated with Apple's iPod device or iTunes music service, they did both contribute to the desire for this capability.
