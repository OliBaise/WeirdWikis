Joblibee


MJollibee is a fast food chain based in the Philippines. There are locations in 6 countries around the world, namely Philippines, United States, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Indonesia and Brunei. Jollibee is also the name of Jollibee's mascot, a big bee in a blazer, shirt and chef's hat. It primarily caters to Filipino taste, and has a Filipino as the CEO. It specializes in chicken, burgers, and spaghetti.
