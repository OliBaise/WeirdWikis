Avicii

Avicii (born as Tim Bergling) is a DJ, remixer, and record producer from Sweden. His most famous hits include Bromance, My Feelings for You and Alcoholic, which all have achieved millions of hits on Youtube.

Biography

When Avicii was new to producing when his friends early in the progress mentioned that his productions were extremely professional. Avicii quickly became fascinated in producing new music and spent many hours doing so. Avicii tried to not fall into any specific house category, but instead wanted his productions to be up to date and innovative. His music is according to himself inspired by Laidback Luke, Steve Angello, Axwell, Daft Punk among many other.
